Eye Care

Eye defects refer to any abnormality or impairment in the structure or function of the eyes that affects vision. Common eye defects include

  • Myopia (nearsightedness)
  • Hyperopia (farsightedness)
  • Astigmatism and presbyopia (age-related loss of near vision)etc.

Eye defects can be caused by genetic factors, environmental factors, or a combination of both. They can often be corrected with glasses, contact lenses, or refractive surgeries like LASIK. Regular eye exams are important to detect and manage eye defects early and prevent further deterioration of vision.

MedCall provides comprehensive eye care solutions to patients (children and adults). With a team of highly skilled eye specialists, MedCall offers a range of services including eye exams, prescription glasses and contact lenses, treatment for eye conditions and diseases, and surgical procedures. The service is accessible through a mobile app or website, allowing patients to schedule appointments, consult with eye doctors, and order eyewear online. MedCall eye care aims to provide convenient and affordable eye care services, ensuring the health and well-being of individuals' vision.
