About me

Eduation:

Dr. Oyewala holds a Doctor of Medicine (MD) degree from Ivy League School of Medicine, where his passion for psychiatry took root. Committed to unraveling the mysteries of the mind, he pursued a Ph.D. in Neuroscience, delving into the interplay between brain function and mental well-being.

Clinical Expertise:

With over 15 years of clinical experience, Dr. Oyewale has become a beacon of expertise in the field. His extensive knowledge spans a wide spectrum of psychiatric conditions, from mood disorders to complex neurodevelopmental issues.

Research and Publications:

Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Oyewale is a prolific researcher and author. His groundbreaking studies on the neural correlates of emotional regulation have been published in leading neuroscience journals. His commitment to advancing the field is evident in his contributions to conferences and symposiums.

Innovative Approaches:

Dr. Oyewale is known for his innovative approaches to mental health treatment. From traditional psychotherapy to cutting-edge neurofeedback and psychopharmacology, he tailors treatment plans to the unique needs of each patient. His integration of holistic wellness principles ensures a comprehensive approach to mental health care.
